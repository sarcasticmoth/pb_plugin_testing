You asked the questions, and EV Industries answered the call. Introducing… Where’s my Wizard?
Where’s my Wizard seeks to bring the lost witch or wizard home. Connect with your friends and their avatar will appear on your own curated Where’s my Wizard map. Covering the entire globe, Where’s my Wizard aims to organize the magical folk of the world so we never have a missing person report again.

Where’s my Wizard consists of a simple interface with two tabs - a friend list display with the option to add friends, and a map display, with locations of all added friends embedded into the map.

Where’s my Wizard is free to download on the WAS today - the first 500 downloads will be entered to win a grand prize of a brand new, state of the art tablet.
