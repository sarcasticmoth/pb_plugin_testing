Having trouble handling your knuts? 

EV Industries can help, now premiering their latest mobile phone application Sickleswap. Now, members of the magical community can send their gold to one another digitally! No more struggling paying for that Deflating Draught and dropping it because your hands were full of knuts. 

Connect with your friends via the easy to use mobile app! The goal of the Sickleswap app is to help the Magical community with everyday transactions and also help local businesses as well - there are no hidden transaction fees to send and receive galleons and the app provides an easy interface for viewing one’s transaction history. For magical beings who have never seen the QWERTY keyboard, or for the struggling wizard busting a knut trying to learn how to use it, the app has a built in panel of emojis for your convenience.
